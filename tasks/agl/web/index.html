Pattern Learning Study

Thank you for participating in this research study on how people learn hidden patterns.

What you'll do

  • View sequences of letters and decide if they follow a hidden rule
  • You'll get feedback at first, then we'll test what you learned
  • The whole study takes about 25-35 minutes

Your data

  • We record your responses, timing, and self-reports
  • All data is de-identified and may be shared publicly for research
  • You can withdraw at any time without penalty

How it works

You'll see letter strings like MXVR or VTXR.

Some strings follow a hidden pattern. Others don't. Your job: decide which is which.

Controls

  • Press Follows Pattern or Doesn't Follow
  • You'll get feedback during learning (correct/incorrect)
  • Every 10 trials, rate how confident you feel
  • If you think you've figured it out, hit the AHA! button

Don't worry if it's hard at first — that's normal. Just do your best.
